This girl always dreamed travelling and living abroad. She grew up to became an interior designer in Australia, living apart from her family for a decade she never thought possible. Now she's imagining again.

This time, as a software developer. She used to design for people affecting their lives through something as simple as color, or as practical as the distance from point A to point B. Now she designs virtually, the same intention, but less specific and wider reach.
